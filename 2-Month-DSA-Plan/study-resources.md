## Practice Platforms

### 1. LeetCode
**URL:** https://leetcode.com/

**How to Use:**
- Start with "Easy" problems
- Filter by topic (Arrays, Strings, etc.)
- Do "Top Interview Questions"
- Participate in weekly contests

**Recommended Lists:**
- LeetCode 75 (Blind 75)
- Top 100 Liked Questions
- Topic-wise explore cards

**Daily Routine:**
- 2-3 Easy problems OR
- 1-2 Medium problems

### 2. Codeforces
**URL:** https://codeforces.com/

**How to Use:**
- Solve problems rated 800-1400

- Participate in contests (even virtually)

#### 3. "Competitive Programming" 
- Authors: Steven & Felix Halim
- Great for problem-solving

- Author: Gayle Laakmann McDowell
- Interview focused
- Good problem collection

## Motivation & Tips

### Stay Consistent
- 3-4 hours daily is better than 10 hours once a week
- Don't skip days
- Even 1 hour is better than 0

### Don't Compare
- Everyone learns at different pace
- Focus on your progress
- Quality > Quantity

### Take Breaks
- Pomodoro technique (25 min work, 5 min break)
- Exercise daily
- Sleep 7-8 hours

### Join Community
- Find study partners
- Share progress on social media
- Participate in contests
